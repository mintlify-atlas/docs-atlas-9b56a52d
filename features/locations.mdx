## Food Pantries (Free Resources)

These locations provide free food and don't require Slug Points.

<Accordion title="Redwood Free Market">
  **Location:** Rachel Carson College Café (formerly College Eight Café)
  
  **Hours:**
  - Monday: 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  - Tuesday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  - Wednesday: 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM (Grad Students Only 2-5 PM)
  - Thursday: 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  - Friday: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  - Saturday-Sunday: Closed

  Self-serve pantry with fresh produce, dry goods, and hygiene items. Choice-based with no limits—take what you need!
  
  [Visit Instagram →](https://www.instagram.com/redwoodfreemarket/)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Cowell Coffee Shop (Free)">
  **Location:** Near Cowell/Stevenson Dining Hall
  
  **Hours:**
  - Monday-Friday: 10:00 AM - 3:30 PM
  - Saturday-Sunday: Closed

  Student-run, non-transactional café serving free soups, curries, baked goods, coffee, salads, and bagels. Just show your UCSC student ID. Bring a reusable mug for hot drinks!
  
  [Visit Linktree →](https://linktr.ee/cowellcoffeeshop)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Women's Center Cultural Pantry">
  **Location:** Cardiff House
  
  **Hours:**
  - Tuesday-Friday: 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM
  - Monday, Saturday-Sunday: Closed

  Year-round free cultural food pantry open to all, fully stocked with pasta, ramen, Japanese curry, rice, oatmeal, beans, soups, sauces, and snacks.
  
  [Learn more →](https://womenscenter.ucsc.edu/resources/cardiff-services/)
</Accordion>

<Note>
  There are many more food pantries on campus including Terry Freitas Commons, Ethnic Resource Center's Food Pantry, Family Student Housing Pantry, Lionel Cantu Queer Center Pantry, SlugCents Pantry, engaging education Nutrition Lounge, and STARRS Personal Care Pantry. Visit the [UCSC Basic Needs website](https://basicneeds.ucsc.edu/) for complete information.
</Note>

## Additional Food Resources

<Accordion title="Meal Swipe Request Form">
  Short-term assistance available to any enrolled student facing immediate food insecurity. Emergency assistance is available up to twice a year through the Swipes for Slugs request form.
  
  [Access the form →](https://basicneeds.ucsc.edu/food-security/on-campus/)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="CalFresh (SNAP Benefits)">
  Eligible students can receive up to $291/month for groceries on an EBT card usable at Porter Market, Merrill Market, Produce Pop-Up, Trader Joe's, Safeway, Costco, and many farmers' markets.
  
  [Apply for CalFresh →](https://basicneeds.ucsc.edu/food-security/calfresh/)
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Produce Pop-Up">
  Shop affordable, organic produce and flowers grown on the UCSC Farm. EBT accepted and doubles up to $10 with Market Match!
  
  **Schedule:**
  - Wednesday 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM at McHenry Library
  - Friday 11:00 AM - 3:00 PM at Science & Engineering Library
  
  [Visit Instagram →](https://www.instagram.com/ucscproducepopup)
</Accordion>

## Using SlugShare with Locations

When creating a donation request on SlugShare, select the dining location where you'll be eating. This helps donors know:
- When you need the points (based on meal times)
- Which location to meet at (if coordinating in person)
- How much you're requesting (based on standard meal pricing)

<Note type="tip">
  Hours may vary by quarter and during finals week. Check [UCSC Dining](https://dining.ucsc.edu/) for the most current schedules.
</Note>
