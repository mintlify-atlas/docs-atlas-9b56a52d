## What is SlugShare?

SlugShare is a community-driven platform that helps UCSC students share dining hall points with each other. Whether you have extra points at the end of the quarter or need help getting a meal, SlugShare connects students to support one another.

<Note>
  SlugShare is built by students, for students. All transactions happen peer-to-peer within the UCSC community.
</Note>

## How It Works

SlugShare makes point sharing simple and transparent:

1. **Create a Request** - Post a request for dining points at any UCSC dining location
2. **Browse Requests** - See what your fellow Slugs need help with
3. **Accept & Donate** - Help someone out by accepting their request
4. **Track Your Balance** - Keep your points updated to stay in control

All dining halls automatically calculate the correct meal swipe cost based on the current meal period (breakfast, lunch, dinner, or late night).

## Supported Locations

SlugShare supports all major UCSC dining locations:

### Dining Halls
- Cowell/Stevenson (Open until 11 PM most days)
- Crown/Merrill (Weekdays only)
- Porter/Kresge (Weekdays until 7 PM)
- Rachel Carson/Oakes (Open until 11 PM most days)
- College 9/John R. Lewis (Open until 11 PM most days)

### Markets
- Merrill Market (Weekdays 9 AM - 8 PM)
- Porter Market (Weekdays 7 AM - 8 PM)

### Perks Coffee Bars
- Baskin Engineering
- Earth and Marine
- Physical Sciences

### Cafes & Restaurants
- Banana Joe's Late Night (8 PM - 11 PM)
- Global Village Cafe
- Oakes Cafe
- Stevenson Coffee House
- University Center Cafe
- Owl's Nest
- University Center Bistro

<Warning>
  Location hours are dynamically shown in the app. Only open locations can be selected when creating requests.
</Warning>

## Dining Hall Pricing

For dining halls with standard pricing, SlugShare automatically calculates the cost based on the current meal period:

| Meal Period | Slug Points Cost |
|-------------|------------------|
| Breakfast | $12.60 |
| Lunch | $13.65 |
| Dinner | $14.70 |
| Late Night | $13.65 |

For markets, cafes, and other locations, you enter the custom point amount you need.

- **Cowell Coffee Shop** - Free meals and drinks for students
- **Produce Pop-Up** - Affordable organic produce from the UCSC Farm

- **NextAuth.js v5** - Secure authentication
- **PostgreSQL + Prisma** - Reliable database
- **shadcn/ui** - Beautiful UI components
